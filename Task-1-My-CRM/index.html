Our Story

More Than Just
a Cup of Coffee

Brew Haven was born in 2019 from a simple dream: to create a space in the heart of Bangalore where people could slow down, breathe, and connect — over a really, really good cup of coffee.

We source our beans directly from small farms in Coorg and Chikmagalur, roasting in small batches to preserve every subtle note. Our chefs bake fresh every morning, and our baristas train for months before they pull their first shot.

Whether you're here to work, to catch up with old friends, or simply to sit with a book — this is your place.
